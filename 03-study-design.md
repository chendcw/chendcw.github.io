## Core Design Frameworks

### 1. Experimental Designs
| Design Type | Application | Considerations |
|------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Parallel RCT** | Gold standard for efficacy | Requires randomization |
| **Crossover** | Chronic conditions | Washout period critical |
| **Factorial** | Multi-interaction studies | Increased sample size needs |
| **Adaptive** | Phase II/III transitions | Complex statistical planning |
